About Me

My name is Antonio and I'm a software engineer from Washington State who love's coding more than it rains!

I started working with Arduino and Raspberry Pi at a young age which translated from hardware to software. I have a fervor for frontend development with React and Redux, and a love of building backends' with Flask and SQL-Alchemy!

I strive to be a team player always uniting diverse people's in order to achieve great things! In my free time, I like to explore the outdoors, write poetry, and work on personal projects. I enjoy leading teams, trying out new things, and working with a unique set of people to reach new heights and grow as a leader and a developer!
